Search for the Essentials

As soon as we land, we should not rush to play differently. We need to go to buildings and search for essentials. Because we can use these essentials for protecting ourselves.

Essential List

Consumable

Vest

Weapon

Bullet

Helmet

Backpack

If other players are keep shooting us and we cannot find ways to hide or run, we should fight back. Otherwise, we will die and play another match again, which is time-consuming. After killing them, do not forget to grab their items to refill essentials.





Do not be Aggressive

As I said in the first part, we should fight back if other players keep shooting us. However, it is better to ignore them and run because we will have more time to play differently.

Find Somewhere Safe

Rock

Building

Tree

Grass

We should use these things to protect ourselves and take consumables. After taking consumables, we need to check around everything is safe and start running again.





Stay in the Circle

A blue wall that is marked on the map slowly closes in so it draws players into a smaller circle as time passes. All the players outside of the blue wall will receive damage. Therefore, we need to pay attention to the blue wall when and where will come even if we enjoy playing PUBG differently.

The Steps of Staying in the Circle

Play PUBG differently
Play attention to the blue wall
If the blue wall is coming, go inside the circle
Stay in the circle and play PUBG differently
Repeat
