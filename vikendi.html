Vikendi - Dino Park

Vikendi is the 4th 6X6 map of PUBG covered in snow ~ where we can see snowy mountains, frozen lakes and small snowy towns. Vikendi has various attractions including the future Cosomodrome, the prehistoric Dino Park, the European Castle and the historic winery.

I love to play PUBG differently in the Dino Park which is located in the left corner. It is a prehistoric-themed park with a wide variety of interesting places such as roller coaster, bumper cars, dino maze, dino museum, gift shops, and dino figures. These are the two items I must play in every matches~

Taking Photos

There are a lot of 1-to-1 scale dinosaurs in the park. Also, do not forget to take photos of the huge volcano! I love to wear different outfits and take selfies in the park because it is difficult to do these things in Hong Kong.

Walking Roller Coaster

Walking? There is no electricity in the park so we need to use our foot to "ride" the roller coaster. Balance and patience are the key thing when we walk the roller coaster. Do not forget to use the largerst dinosaur brachiosaurus to slide to the floor. Otherwise, we will die beacause of jumping from the top.
