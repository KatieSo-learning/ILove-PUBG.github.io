My Favourite Outfit

Ruby Fairy Outfit

Uniqueness:
Cuteness:

There was a Event called "Total Sign-in". I had to sign in the game every day for more than a month to get the fairy outfit<. To be honest, I do not play PUBG every day because you know work life balance~ Therefore, I set an alarm to remind me to log in and play PUBG every day for the outfit.

Frog Tourist Outfit

Uniqueness:
Cuteness:

There was a event called "GB Teddy Bear". I had to make 17 bears to get the free frog outfit. Making bears was challenging because it needed 6 pieces of cotton to make a bear. It means I need to find more than 100 pieces of cotton. I remember I spent a month to make bears and get the outfit.

Blue Rabbit Outfit

Uniqueness:
Cuteness:

PUBG has 9 Ranking Tiers. I had to reach the Gold Tier and finish in Top 10 5 times in Gold Tier to get the rabbit outfit. I love to play PUBG differently so it is challenging to be aggressive and kill other players. However, I really wanted to get the outfit. Therefore, I stop playing differently to reach higher points.

Buying Outfit Tips

If we want to buy the special outfits like the hat collection I mentioned, we can use special sliver coins to buy them on store. However, we do not top up so we need to use BP coins exchange special silver coins.

Here is the tutorial gallery~ Please also read the Quick Tips

Quick Tips

BP coins are earned from matches
The exchange daily limit is 25 silver coins
Choose the items period (7d, 30d, Permanent) carefully
Exchange silver coins every day





Free Outfit Tips

PUBG has a event called "Welcome Back Event". If we complete the return mission in PUBG, PUBG will give us many gifts such as backpack and the most important one outfit (That's how I got corn outfit) for thanking us for return.

Return Misson

Log out and delete the PUBG app
Wait at least 25 days
Download the PUBG app and log in
The "Welcome Back Event" will appear
Sign-in PUBG for 8 days
Get the free outfit and items
Repeat the above steps to get more free outfit and items

Return 8 Day Sign-in
